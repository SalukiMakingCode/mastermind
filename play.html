Welcome to the Mastermind Challenge

You will have to finish the 10 levels to win the game. The difficulty will increase every level.

The aim of the game is to guess the position of each colour in the combination. Each time you propose a combination, you will see black and white pieces appear. Black means that you have a well placed pawn, white means that the pawn exists but is not well placed. But these pieces do not tell you which colour is well or badly placed!

You have some bonuses to complete the game, but use them wisely, they are limited for the whole game.
